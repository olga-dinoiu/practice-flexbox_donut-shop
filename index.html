Who we are

The Donut shop opened in 1986 as a family owned and operated establishment. Our family continues this tradition to this day, dedicated to preserving the art of making handmade donuts. We are proud to be one of the last independent donut shops that still make our donuts and pastries from scratch.

Our donuts have a classic taste and texture achieved using old world techniques.
